Lasagna

Try our favourite beef lasagna recipe, you will not regret the end product!

20 minutes prep | 1 hour 20 minutes cook | Serves 8

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 2 brown onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 750g beef mince
  • 2 x 400g cans Italian diced tomatoes
  • 125ml cup dry red wine
  • 55g tomato paste
  • salt & freshly ground black pepper
  • 55g grated mozzarella cheese blocks
  • 4 fresh lasagna sheets
  • mixed salad leaves, to serve
  • 1L milk
  • 8 fresh parsley stalks
  • 8 whole black peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 60g butter
  • 50g plain flour
  • 70g finely grated parmesan

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in frying pan over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic to cook and stir for 5 minutes. Add the mince and stir with a wooden spoon to break up any lumps, for 5 minutes or until mince changes colour. Add tomato, wine and tomato paste and bring to the boil. Reduce the heat to low.
  2. Simmer, uncovered, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes or until sauce thickens slightly. Remove the heat and season with salt and pepper
  3. For the cheese sauce, combine the milk, onion, parsley stalks, peppercorns and bay leaves in a medium saucepan and bring to a simmer over medium heat. Remove from heat and set aside for 15 minutes to infuse
  4. Strain the milk mixture through a fine sieve into a large jug. Discard solids
  5. Melt the butter in a large saucepan over medium-high heat until foaming. Add the flour and cook, stirring until mixture bubbles and begins to come away from the side of the pan. Remove from heat
  6. Gradually pour in half the milk, whisking constantly with a balloon whisk, until mixture is smooth. Gradually add the remaining milk, whisking until smooth and combined
  7. Place saucepan over medium-high heat and bring to the boil, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon, for 5 minutes or until sauce thickens and coats the back of the spoon. Remove the heat. Add the parmesan and stir until cheese melts
  8. Preheat oven to 180 degrees celcius. Brush a rectangular 3L capacity dish with oil to lightly grease. Spread one-quarter of the bechamel sauce over the base of the prepared dish. Arrange 1 lasagna sheet over the sauce. Top with one-third of the mince mixture and one-third of the remaining bechamel sauce. Continue layering with remaining lasagna sheets. Sprinkle with mozzarella
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 40 minutes or until cheese melts and is golden brown. Remove from oven and set aside for 10 minutes to set
  10. Cut the lasagna into 8 portions and serve with mixed salad leaves.
